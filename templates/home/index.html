Community-rooted baseball in Vancouver

Vancouver Minor Baseball is a not-for-profit association powered by volunteers, alumni, and partners who believe baseball should be accessible, inspiring, and fun for every athlete. We focus on culture first, pairing elite instruction with inclusive programming.

From Nanaimo Park to showcase tournaments across North America, our teams compete with pride while giving back through mentorship clinics, community service, and grassroots fundraising.

Fast facts

  • 1999 — Founded and joined BC Minor Baseball
  • 350+ active players across four divisions
  • 15 certified coaches & skills specialists
  • 12 provincial & tournament titles since 2020
